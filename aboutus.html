About Us

Every day, more than 11 million guests visit Testy Bites restaurants around the world. And they do so because our restaurants are
known for serving high-quality, great-tasting, and affordable food. Founded in 2000, Testy Bites is the fourth largest fast Kottu
hamburger chain in the world. The original Home of the Whopper, our commitment to premium ingredients, signature recipes, and
family-friendly dining experiences is what has defined our brand for more than 24 successful years.
